Semester Project Idea:

An idea I had for my semester project was a website catered to those who are interested in the retro game market and are interested in a way to make collecting older games more convenient, and may help them find more options than just looking on amazon or ebay. It would allow the user to search for a particular game, and instead of focusing where it can be bought online, it can help locate the game of their choice at a retro game store. As a retro game collector myself, I much prefer going to a store instead of relying on the internet. This project can help users find out if a store is carrying a particular game through postings by the store themselves through the site, and the results can come through a search. Some retro game stores are privately owned, and may not even have a website of their own, and at the most may only have a social media page to post the games they may have gotten through trading and the like. This project can also be another way for the smaller stores to make themselves known, and users may discover a store they may not have known existed in their area. If it's too ambitious, I may change the idea a tad to maybe have it serve it as a method of discovering retro game stores in one's area, instead of having postings of games that a store may have. Might make other changes as time goes on, but this is where I am in my thinkng process.
